Who We Are

Rooted in Faith & Purpose

Word & Life Empowerment Ministry is committed to transforming children and youth through mentorship, discipleship, life skills, trauma healing, and Biblical teaching.

We reach into remote communities where children need God's love most, working through trained teachers, church leaders, and dedicated volunteers.

🎯

Our Mission

Empowering children and youth through God's Word, mentorship, life skills, and healing for holistic transformation.

🔭

Our Vision

To see a generation of children and youth who are healed, confident, rooted in God's Word, and living transformed lives.

Our Leadership

Meet Our Founder

Founder & Director

Pastor Paul Masilikali

Word & Life Empowerment Ministry

Pastor Paul Masilikali is the visionary founder of Word & Life Empowerment Ministry. With a deep passion for children and youth, he has dedicated his life to reaching the most vulnerable communities in Malawi with the transformative power of God's Word.

Through his leadership, the ministry has grown to reach over 2,000 children, trained hundreds of pastors and teachers, and established Bible study groups across multiple communities.

"Every child is a gift from God, and it is our responsibility to nurture them in faith, equip them with skills, and empower them to become leaders who will transform their communities."
