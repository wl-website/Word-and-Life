Overview

Equipping Teachers for Impact

Teachers from churches and communities gather for intensive training sessions to learn effective methods of teaching children and leading Bible studies — practical, faith-centered, and immediately applicable.

Topics Covered

🔊

Audio Bible Teaching

Bringing Scripture alive using audio tools for all learning styles.

📖

Storytelling Bible Studies

Narrative methods that make Bible stories memorable.

👧

Child-Centered Teaching

Participatory approaches honouring children as learners.

🤝

Mentorship Skills

Building meaningful relationships with children.

✟

Bible Study Facilitation

Creating dynamic, participatory group Bible study.
